About Us

UoLCS Feedback is created and run by a team of University of London students enrolled in the Computer Science BSc online-only degree programme. Enrolled in this innovative yet almost experimental degree, we - along with our fellow students - were united in our common challenges of exploring and navigating the online-only curriculum. We observed that the University of London-supplied material about the degree specifications were not always enough for our fellow students (and us!) to decide among the many possible paths of taking the necessary courses. We personally felt that there had to be a better way to help students familiarize themselves with the courses that lay in front of them before enrolling in them. We finally decided what better resource for this than the feedback of other students who have already taken those courses!

Our team consists of students with skills varying from back-end programming to graphic design. Feel free to contact us at uolcsfeedback@gmail.com.

UoLCS Feedback is an independent project and is neither affiliated with nor endorsed by the University of London.

We strive for high quality reviews that address the actual experiences from current students or alumni.
